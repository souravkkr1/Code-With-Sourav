About Us

We Believe In A Clean, Sustainable Environment

We are a group of like-minded technologists, advisors, developers, and Islamic scholars who believe in utilizing technology to improve people's lives worldwide, regardless of race, color, or creed. Our goal is to provide education, resources, and support to foster the use of AradChain’s Blockchain-powered technology for social good so we can all tackle some of the world's most pressing social and environmental issues, food wastage, global hunger and poverty.
