Our Mission

Our

Mission

AradChain was established to provide a decentralized blockchain solution for agricultural supply chains where local farmers, oracles, and entrepreneurs can sell and buy directly without any intermediaries. Our goals are to improve opportunities for all in the region by promoting local economic development as well as social and community building through educational and friendly activities

Our

Vision

The AradChain stablish to provide blockchain base supply chain solutions to corporate and individuals especially in the field of small-scale agriculture and specialty products to get easy access to a marketplace for all stakeholders in a centralized way without the help of any intermediaries. We aim to promote the local production of agricultural products and specialty products in the specific region and create business opportunities. With the help of a blockchain base ecosystem, we strive to improve the variety, taste, and freshness of products available to customers in the region. We aim to promote strong, trusting relationships between consumers and vendors by insisting on honesty and transparency in all aspects of the creation of products. We create a space for interaction between the consumer and the producer. We believe that there are inherent benefits for the customer, producer, and community when food is grown, purchased, and consumed locally.

Our Intention Of Creating Borderless Synergy Throughout The Global Farming And Food

By implementing sustainable farming practices we aim to ensure the industry's long-term health and protect the environment. We aim to encourage fair trade and equitable distribution of resources and profits throughout the industry and synergize partnerships between industries in different countries to share knowledge and resources.
