Our

Mission

An important part of our initiative is to promote strong, trusting relationships between consumers and vendors by insisting on honesty and transparency in all aspects of the creation of products and encouraging a healthy space of interaction between the customer and the producer. On a daily basis, we strive to improve opportunities for all in any given region by promoting local economic development as well as social and community building through educational and friendly activities.

Our

Vision

AradChain was established to provide a range of fully comprehensive blockchain, A.I and IoT supply chain solutions to governments, corporates and of course, individuals. Our goal is to provide a decentralized blockchain solution for agricultural supply chains where local farmers, oracles, and entrepreneurs can sell and buy directly without any intermediaries. We aim to promote the local production of agricultural products. And with the help of a blockchain-based ecosystem, we strive to improve the variety, taste, and freshness of products available to customers in a given region.
